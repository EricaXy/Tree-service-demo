How Claude and Gemini work together

Claude handles everything that requires language, judgment, and conversation. Gemini handles everything that requires touching data files and Google's ecosystem. They hand off in the middle — Claude tells Gemini what to write to the Sheet, Gemini tells Claude the numbers for the weekly report.

The client never sees any of this. They just get a WhatsApp message saying they're live, a link to their read-only Google Sheet, and a Friday email every week showing how many jobs got revived.

How Claude personalises each message

Claude reads the lead data (name, job type, property, quote date) from Google Sheets and rewrites each template before it goes out. No two messages are identical. A stump grinding quote gets a different tone than an emergency storm removal — urgency, seasonality, and job size all factor in.
